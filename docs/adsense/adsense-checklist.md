AdSense Rejection Fix Checklist

1. Content quality
- Publish 20+ original articles (500–800+ words each).
- Remove thin/scraped content. Proofread and add images and internal links.

2. Required pages
- Add Privacy Policy, About, Contact, Terms and link them in footer/main nav.

3. Site completeness & UX
- Fix broken links, ensure clear navigation, mobile-friendly, fast loading.

4. Verify ownership & crawlability
- Add site to Google Search Console and verify.
- Ensure robots.txt and sitemap are correct; don’t block Googlebot.

5. Traffic & sources
- Use organic/legit traffic sources; avoid paid/incentivized traffic.

6. Policy compliance
- Remove copyrighted/adult/prohibited content and deceptive layouts.

7. Domain age & timing
- If domain is new, wait (some regions expect ~6 months).

Reapply process
- Fix issues, wait 1–3 weeks for recrawl/indexing, then reapply.
- If rejected again, post URL to AdSense Help Community for feedback.
